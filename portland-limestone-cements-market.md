<p><p>The Portland limestone cements market is divided into two main types based on consistency: standard consistency and extra thick consistency. Standard consistency cements exhibit a balanced fluidity, making them suitable for a wide range of applications, including general construction and precast elements. In contrast, extra thick consistency cements are characterized by their higher viscosity, which provides enhanced workability and stability in demanding environments, such as heavy-duty construction or specific architectural projects. Both types cater to varying performance requirements in construction.</p></p>
