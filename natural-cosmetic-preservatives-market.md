<p><p>The natural cosmetic preservatives market is categorized into four main types: lipids, acids, alcohols, and others. Lipids, such as plant oils, provide moisture while inhibiting microbial growth. Acids, like citric and sorbic acid, offer effective pH control and preservation. Alcohols, including ethanol and glycerin, serve as antimicrobial agents and enhance product stability. The "others" category encompasses various natural extracts and compounds that help maintain the integrity and safety of cosmetic formulations while appealing to health-conscious consumers.</p></p>
