<p><ul><li>Semi-automatic Systems</li><li>Fully-automatic Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automatic Reticle Storage Systems are categorized into two main types: semi-automatic and fully-automatic systems. Semi-automatic systems require some manual input for operation, enabling users to select and adjust reticles as needed, offering a balance between user control and automation. In contrast, fully-automatic systems streamline the process entirely, automatically storing and retrieving reticles without user intervention. This enhances efficiency and speed, making them ideal for high-volume applications where quick access to a variety of reticles is essential.</p></p>
