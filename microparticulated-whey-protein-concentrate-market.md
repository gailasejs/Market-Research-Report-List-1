<p><p>The microparticulated whey protein concentrate market features three primary applications: emulsion, foam stabilization, and fat mimicking. Emulsions use microparticulated whey to enhance the stability and texture of food products by preventing separation of oil and water phases. Foam stabilization involves using these proteins to improve the structure and longevity of foams in products like dairy and desserts. Fat mimicking allows whey proteins to replicate the creamy texture and mouthfeel of fats, making low-fat formulations more appealing without compromising taste.</p></p>
