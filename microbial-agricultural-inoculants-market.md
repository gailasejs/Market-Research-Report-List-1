<p><ul><li>Soil inoculation</li><li>Seed inoculation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The microbial agricultural inoculants market encompasses two primary types: soil inoculation and seed inoculation. Soil inoculation involves introducing beneficial microorganisms directly into the soil to enhance nutrient availability, improve soil structure, and promote plant growth. Seed inoculation, on the other hand, involves coating seeds with specific microbes before planting to establish beneficial microbial activity from the outset, aiding in seed germination and plant resilience. Both methods aim to boost agricultural productivity and sustainability by harnessing the power of microbes.</p></p>
