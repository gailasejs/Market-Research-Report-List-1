<p><strong>The Gene Expression Profiling Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>RNA Sequencing</li><li>Real-Time Quantitative PCR</li><li>Digital PCR</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gene expression profiling involves various market types, primarily RNA sequencing, real-time quantitative PCR (qPCR), and digital PCR. RNA sequencing provides comprehensive insights into the transcriptome, allowing for the analysis of gene expression at a high resolution. Real-time qPCR offers quantitative measurement of specific RNA targets, enabling detection of changes in expression levels. Digital PCR enhances sensitivity and precision by partitioning samples, ensuring accurate quantification of low-abundance transcripts. Together, these techniques drive advancements in research and clinical applications in genomics.</p></p>
