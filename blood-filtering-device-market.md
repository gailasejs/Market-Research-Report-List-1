<p><p>The blood filtering device market comprises various types, including hemodialysis membranes, leukocyte removal filters, and plasma separation membranes. Hemodialysis membranes are used to remove waste products from the blood in patients with kidney failure, while leukocyte removal filters focus on eliminating white blood cells to reduce the risk of immune reactions during transfusions. Plasma separation membranes facilitate the separation of plasma from blood cells for therapeutic applications, enhancing treatment outcomes in various medical scenarios. Each type serves specific clinical needs.</p></p>
