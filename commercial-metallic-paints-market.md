<p><ul><li>Solvent Based</li><li>Water Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial metallic paints market is segmented into solvent-based and water-based types. Solvent-based metallic paints utilize organic solvents as carriers, offering durability, high gloss, and excellent adhesion, making them suitable for industrial applications. In contrast, water-based metallic paints use water as a solvent, providing easier application, lower VOC emissions, and reduced environmental impact. They are often preferred for interior applications due to their quick drying times and lower odor, catering to diverse customer preferences across various sectors.</p></p>
