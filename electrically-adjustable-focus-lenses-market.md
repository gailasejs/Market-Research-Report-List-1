<p><p>The Electrically Adjustable Focus Lenses market includes two main types: electrowetting-based liquid lenses and liquid crystal lenses. Electrowetting-based lenses utilize electrical signals to manipulate liquid interfaces, allowing rapid focus adjustments, ideal for cameras and imaging systems. Liquid crystal lenses employ liquid crystal technology to alter the refractive index through electric voltage, enabling precise focus control in optical devices. Both types enhance optical performance and adaptability in applications such as smartphones, autonomous vehicles, and virtual reality.</p></p>
