<p><p>The automotive data cables market encompasses various types of communications technologies essential for vehicle connectivity. Coaxial cables support high-frequency signals for video and audio. FlexRay offers deterministic data communication for advanced driver-assistance systems. Controller Area Network Flexible Data-Rate (CAN-FD) and Controller Area Network (CAN) are critical for real-time communication between vehicle components. Additional cables include emerging technologies tailored for specific applications.</p></p>
