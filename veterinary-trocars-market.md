<p><ul><li>Orthopedic</li><li>Thoracic</li><li>Laparoscopic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The veterinary trocars market encompasses three primary types: orthopedic, thoracic, and laparoscopic. Orthopedic trocars are designed for joint and bone surgeries, facilitating stable access to musculoskeletal structures. Thoracic trocars are specialized for surgeries involving the thoracic cavity, aiding in procedures such as pleural drainage. Laparoscopic trocars enable minimally invasive surgeries by providing access to the abdominal cavity with reduced recovery time and discomfort. Each type serves distinct veterinary surgical needs, enhancing procedural efficiency and animal welfare.</p></p>
