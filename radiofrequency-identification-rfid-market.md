<p><p>Radiofrequency Identification (RFID) technology uses electromagnetic fields to automatically identify and track tags attached to objects. The RFID market is categorized into three types based on frequency: Low Frequency (LF, 125-134 kHz), which is ideal for animal tracking and access control; High Frequency (HF, 13.56 MHz), commonly used in libraries and payment systems; and Ultra-High Frequency (UHF, 300 MHz to 3 GHz), suitable for inventory management and supply chain applications. Each type offers unique advantages for various industries.</p></p>
