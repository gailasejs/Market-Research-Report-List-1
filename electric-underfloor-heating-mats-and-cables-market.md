<p><p>Electric underfloor heating systems primarily consist of heating mats and heating cables. Heating mats are pre-fabricated sheets that contain heating elements embedded in them, making installation easier and quicker, often preferred for smaller or complex areas. In contrast, heating cables are flexible wires that can be laid out in varying patterns, providing more design versatility for larger spaces or custom layouts. Both options offer energy-efficient warmth by evenly distributing heat across floors, enhancing comfort in residential and commercial settings.</p></p>
