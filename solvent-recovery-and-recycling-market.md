<p><p>The Solvent Recovery and Recycling market comprises two main types: on-site and off-site solvent recycling. On-site solvent recycling involves the collection and purification of solvents directly at the user’s location, allowing for immediate reuse and minimal transportation costs. Off-site solvent recycling, on the other hand, entails the transportation of used solvents to a specialized facility for recovery and reprocessing, often yielding higher purity levels and enabling economies of scale. Both methods aim to reduce waste and promote sustainability in industrial processes.</p></p>
