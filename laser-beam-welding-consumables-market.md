<p><strong>The Laser-Beam Welding Consumables Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Stick Electrodes</li><li>Solid Wires</li><li>Flux Cored Wires</li><li>SAW Wires & Fluxes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laser-beam welding consumables market encompasses various types including stick electrodes, solid wires, flux cored wires, submerged arc welding (SAW) wires, and fluxes. Stick electrodes are used for manual welding processes, providing a versatile option. Solid wires offer clean and efficient welding in automated systems. Flux cored wires incorporate a core that enhances weld quality and productivity. SAW wires are designed for high deposition rates in submerged arc welding. Fluxes are used to improve weld penetration and protect against oxidation during the welding process.</p></p>
