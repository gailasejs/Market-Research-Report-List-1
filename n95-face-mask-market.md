<p><p>The N95 face mask market is primarily divided into two types: cup-shaped and folding N95 masks. Cup N95 masks have a rigid structure, maintaining their shape and providing a secure seal around the face, ideal for industrial use. In contrast, folding N95 masks are lightweight and can be easily stored flat, making them more convenient for everyday use or in situations where space is limited. Both types offer high filtration efficiency, essential for protection against airborne pathogens and particles.</p></p>
