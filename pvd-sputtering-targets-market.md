<p><p>The PVD sputtering targets market can be categorized into two primary types: metal sputtering targets and compound sputtering targets. Metal sputtering targets consist of pure metal materials used for depositing conductive layers in various applications, such as electronics and optics. In contrast, compound sputtering targets are made from mixtures of different materials, like ceramics or alloys, which enable the deposition of specialized coatings with enhanced properties. Both types are crucial in producing thin films with specific characteristics tailored to various industries.</p></p>
