<p><ul><li>Water Based</li><li>Solvent Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Fabric Protection Agent market is primarily categorized into water-based and solvent-based types. Water-based fabric protectors are eco-friendly and safe for indoor use, effectively repelling stains and liquids while allowing breathability. In contrast, solvent-based agents offer robust protection through a durable coating but may contain chemicals that can be harmful to indoor air quality. Both types serve the common purpose of safeguarding textiles from damage, but they differ in application methods, environmental impact, and drying times.</p></p>
