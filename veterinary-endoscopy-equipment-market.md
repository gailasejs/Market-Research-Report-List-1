<p><p>Veterinary endoscopy equipment encompasses various types tailored for diagnostic and therapeutic procedures in animals. Flexible endoscopy uses a flexible tube with a camera and light source, allowing for minimally invasive examinations of internal organs. Rigid endoscopy employs a straight, inflexible tube, ideal for specific anatomical areas. Capsule endoscopy involves swallowing a small, camera-equipped capsule for internal imaging, useful for gastrointestinal evaluations. Other equipment may include specialty scopes for diverse procedures, emphasizing versatility in veterinary diagnostics and treatment.</p></p>
