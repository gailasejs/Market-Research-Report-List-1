<p><ul><li>Oligonucleotide DNA Microarrays (oDNA)</li><li>Complementary DNA Microarrays (cDNA)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>DNA microarrays in agriculture are tools used for gene expression analysis and genetic screening. Oligonucleotide DNA microarrays (oDNA) use short, synthetic DNA probes to detect specific sequences, allowing for precise monitoring of gene activity. Complementary DNA microarrays (cDNA) utilize cDNA synthesized from mRNA, facilitating the analysis of expressed genes in various tissues. Both types play a crucial role in crop improvement, disease resistance studies, and understanding plant responses to environmental changes, ultimately enhancing agricultural productivity and sustainability.</p></p>
