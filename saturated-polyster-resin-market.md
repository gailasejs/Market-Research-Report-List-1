<p><p>The saturated polyester resin market is primarily divided into two types: liquid and solid saturated polyester resins. Liquid saturated polyester resins are widely used in coatings, adhesives, and sealants due to their excellent flow properties and versatility. They offer ease of application and quick drying times. In contrast, solid saturated polyester resins are typically used in applications requiring higher mechanical strength and durability, such as in the production of molded parts and electrical insulation materials. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
