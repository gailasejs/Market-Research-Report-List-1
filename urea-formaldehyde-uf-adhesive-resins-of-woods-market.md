<p><p>Urea-Formaldehyde (UF) adhesive resins for wood are primarily categorized into two types: Type I and Type II. Type I resins are designed for interior applications and offer moderate moisture resistance, making them suitable for products used in dry environments. In contrast, Type II resins are formulated for exterior applications, providing enhanced moisture resistance and durability against outdoor conditions. These distinctions influence their usage in furniture, cabinetry, and construction, catering to varying environmental demands and performance requirements.</p></p>
