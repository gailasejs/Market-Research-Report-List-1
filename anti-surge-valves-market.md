<p><p>The anti-surge valves market comprises three main types: pneumatic, hydraulic, and electric anti-surge valves. Pneumatic anti-surge valves utilize compressed air to control flow, ensuring rapid response during surging conditions. Hydraulic anti-surge valves use fluid pressure to manage operation, offering enhanced precision in high-pressure environments. Electric anti-surge valves operate through electric motors, allowing for automated control and monitoring. Each type is designed to prevent compressor surging, improving system stability and efficiency in various industrial applications.</p></p>
