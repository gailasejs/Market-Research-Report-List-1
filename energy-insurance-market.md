<p><p>The energy insurance market encompasses various specialized types designed to address industry-specific risks. Product liability insurance protects companies from claims related to defective energy products. Multinational casualty insurance covers international operations against liabilities arising from accidents or injuries, ensuring global compliance and protection. Excess casualty insurance offers additional coverage beyond the limits of primary policies, safeguarding against significant financial losses resulting from catastrophic events.
