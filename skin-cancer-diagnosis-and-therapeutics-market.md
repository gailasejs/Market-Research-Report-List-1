<p><p>The skin cancer diagnosis and therapeutics market consists of various types, including Actinic Keratoses (AK), Basal Cell Carcinoma (BCC), Squamous Cell Carcinoma (SCC), and Melanoma. AK represents precancerous lesions leading to potential cancer development. BCC is the most common form, characterized by slow growth and low metastasis. SCC is more aggressive, arising from squamous cells and having higher metastatic potential. Melanoma is the deadliest type, originating from melanocytes, requiring advanced therapeutic approaches for effective management and diagnosis.</p></p>
