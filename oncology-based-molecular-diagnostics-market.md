<p><p>Oncology-based molecular diagnostics comprise various technologies used for cancer detection and monitoring. PCR (Polymerase Chain Reaction) amplifies specific DNA sequences, enabling precise cancer identification. Multiplex PCR allows simultaneous analysis of multiple targets, enhancing efficiency. In Situ Hybridization enables visualization of genetic material within tissue samples, providing crucial insights into tumor characteristics. Isothermal Nucleic Acid Amplification Technology (INAAT) offers rapid nucleic acid amplification at constant temperatures, facilitating quick results in clinical settings. Together, these methods advance cancer diagnosis and treatment.</p></p>
