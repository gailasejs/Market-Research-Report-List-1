<p><p>The Nylon 6 filament yarn market includes several types: Flat, POY (Partially Oriented Yarn), FDY (Fully Drawn Yarn), and HSO (High Strength Oriented). Flat yarn is produced flat for applications requiring a smooth finish. POY is partially drawn, allowing for further processing into FDY, which is fully drawn and offers higher strength and uniformity. HSO is engineered for enhanced strength and durability, making it ideal for demanding applications. Each type serves distinct needs in the textile and manufacturing industries.</p></p>
